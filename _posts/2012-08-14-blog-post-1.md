Hi! Welcome to my very first Waiver Raider Blog post. I am very excited to share my thoughts on fantasy football and sports analytics with you. This blog and work is born out of an embarrassing fantasy football loss in 2023. I've played fantasy football for more than a decade and have never won a championship. I've come close a few times, but I've never been able to get over the hump. 

In 2023, I was invited to join a new league with some of my buddies from high school. This wasn't really a new league. This league is 18 years old, and I was a founding member back when my friends and I were in high school. I was kicked out of the league in college because I would frequently forget to set my line-ups. Now, in my defense, this was before I had a smart phone and could set lineups anywhere. I was just excited to be back in the league with my buddies.

 I was asked last minute to join the league and immediately committed without asking simple questions like "What is the scoring structure?" and "Are there punishments for last place?". This was my first mistake.
